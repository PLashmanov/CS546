About FRAP

Empowering Users in the Fight Against Fraud

FRAP is a cutting-edge application designed to tackle the ever-evolving challenge of fraud. Our platform is a user-driven hub for fraud detection and prevention, providing both real-time alerts and historical insights into fraudulent activities.

Key Features:

  • Crowd-Sourced Fraud Database: Leveraging the power of community, our app offers a comprehensive, continuously updated database of fraud instances, sourced from users worldwide.
  • Real-Time Fraud Reporting: Stay ahead of fraudsters with our real-time reporting feature. This enables immediate sharing and receiving of alerts about potential fraudulent activities.
  • Historical Records: Knowledge is power. We provide access to aggregated historical fraud records from various sources, allowing users to spot trends and protect themselves against recurrent scams.
